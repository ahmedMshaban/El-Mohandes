Small drilling rig for soil investigation

We build small rigs for many applications.

The shown rig is core drilling rig for standard 3 meter drill rod sizes 55.6,69.9 &88.9 mm casing up to 8 inch.

  • Max rotation :450 RPM
  • Max. Torque : 2100 N.M
  • Mast total length 5.7 M
  • Hydraulic winch 1.3 Ton

Major function

  • 4 Stabilizer cylinder
  • Mast tilting cylinder
  • Power head up / down cylinder
  • Power head with swivel joint
  • Hydraulic winch

Special functions

  • Mast support hydraulic cylinder
  • Pipe tong for drill
  • Pipe breakout
  • Pull up &feed down control
  • Drilling rpm control

The rig can be supplied on 2 axle trailer , or single axle trailer ,also as skit to be mounted over truck.

We can add any additional requirements functions
